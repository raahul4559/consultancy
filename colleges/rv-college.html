ADMISSIONS

RV University admissions are transparent and merit-driven. B.Tech is admitted through JEE Main, postgraduate programmes through KMAT / PGCET, and applications are made directly on the RVU admissions portal.

UG

B.Tech via JEE Main · CET Code E285

PG

MBA via KMAT / equivalent · CET Code B413

PG

M.Tech via PGCET / equivalent · CET Code T615

Applications are submitted on admissions.rvu.edu.in. Ph.D. admissions 2026 are open for full-time and part-time scholars — application deadline 31 March 2026.

Why Choose RV University?

1

A deep-rooted legacy of academic excellence spanning eight decades through RV Educational Institutions (RVEI) — a network of 27 institutions and 8 lakh+ alumni worldwide.

2

Forward-looking, interdisciplinary curriculum with a wide selection of majors and minors across engineering, liberal arts, business, law, design, media and healthcare.

A recognised State Private University (Karnataka Act No. 11 of 2019), recognised by the UGC, with a Bar Council of India–approved School of Law.

4

A strong research culture — 23 active research centres, 290+ publications (2021–25) and 102 PhD scholars across seven schools.

5

A dual-campus experience across Bengaluru and the upcoming Mysuru campus, with merit-based scholarships and international mobility programmes.
