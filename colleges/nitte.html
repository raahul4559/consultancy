Why KCET Quota is Important at NMIT

KCET is the primary admission gateway for engineering seats in Karnataka.

About KCET

1

Conducted by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA).

2

Karnataka government quota — around 45% of seats.

3

Students must be Karnataka domicile or meet KEA eligibility.

4

Rank-based centralized counselling ensures transparency.

Benefits of KCET Quota

1

Lowest fee compared to COMEDK or management quota.

2

Merit-based allocation ensures transparency.

3

Ideal for students with average or moderate ranks.

4

Affordable quality engineering education.

Why COMEDK Quota is Important at NMIT

National-level entrance for private engineering colleges in Karnataka.

About COMEDK

1

Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka.

2

Around 30% of seats are filled through the COMEDK quota.

3

Open to students across India — no Karnataka domicile required.

4

Admission is based on rank + seat availability.

Benefits of COMEDK Quota

1

Access to top colleges for non-Karnataka students.

2

Wider opportunity pool with all-India competition.

3

Rank-based counselling ensures fairness and transparency.

4

Strong placement and academic record across COMEDK colleges.
