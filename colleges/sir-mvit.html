Eligibility Criteria for B.Tech 2026

✓
Class 12 / Equivalent

CBSE, ISC, IB, Cambridge or State Board (PUC) with Physics, Chemistry / Biology, Mathematics.

✓
Minimum Aggregate

45–50% aggregate in PCM / PCB for general category; relaxations apply for SC/ST and reserved categories.

✓
Compulsory Subjects

Physics & Mathematics mandatory; plus Chemistry / Biology / Computer Science / Biotechnology.

✓
Entrance Exam

Valid KCET, COMEDK UGET, JEE Main rank, or direct / management channel based on programme.

Nationality
