01 Who We Are

Binayak Consultancy is an independent educational guidance and counseling firm based in Bengaluru, India. We help students and families understand engineering admissions in India — primarily Karnataka — through one-to-one counseling, college research, and information sharing.

Everything published on this website is intended as general guidance and educational information. It is not a substitute for direct verification with the colleges, universities, or counseling authorities involved.

02 Guidance, Not Admission

We are not the admission office, official representative, agent, or affiliate of any college or university referred to on this website, unless we have stated that explicitly. Logos, names and information about institutions appear here only to help students compare and decide.

Our role is limited to guidance and counseling:

  • Sharing information about colleges, courses, eligibility, fees, and historical cutoff trends
  • Personalized counseling on course choice, campus fit, and application strategy
  • Helping students prepare documents and understand counseling rounds
  • Pointing students to the correct official portals and authorities
We do not issue admission letters, allot seats, collect institutional fees, or guarantee a place in any college. Those decisions are made entirely by the colleges and the relevant counseling authorities.

03 No Guaranteed Outcomes

Admission to any college, university or program depends on factors that are outside our control, including:

  • Your entrance exam scores, academic record, and overall profile
  • Seat availability, category quotas, and institutional reservation rules
  • Year-on-year changes in cutoffs, eligibility, and counseling rules
  • Decisions of the institution and the relevant counseling authority (e.g. KEA, COMEDK, etc.)

Any opinion, estimate, or shortlist we share is our best-effort guidance and should not be treated as a promise of admission.

04 Accuracy of Information

We make a sincere effort to keep college fees, cutoffs, placements, course details, and related information accurate and current. However, this information is sourced from public records, prior years' counseling data, and our own research — and it changes frequently.

Before you take any decision based on what you read here, please:

  • Verify directly with the college's official admissions website
  • Cross-check with the official counseling portal (KEA, COMEDK, JoSAA, CSAB, etc.)
  • Confirm fee structures and seat matrices for the current academic year

Binayak Consultancy is not liable for losses, missed opportunities, or other consequences arising from reliance on outdated or incorrect information presented on this site.

05 Predictor Tools & Estimates

Tools such as the College Predictor and any rank/cutoff estimates published on this website are indicative only. They are based on previous years' published cutoff data and are intended to help students shortlist options for further consideration.

Predictor results are not a confirmation of admission. Final admission depends entirely on the official counseling rounds and the rules in force in that admission year.
