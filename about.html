About Binayak Consultancy

Binayak Consultancy is an independent engineering-admissions consultancy helping students and parents navigate B.E. / B.Tech admissions across Karnataka's top colleges with clarity and confidence.

We simplify a complex journey — branch selection, KCET / COMEDK / JEE Main and management-quota pathways, documentation and counselling — with honest, transparent guidance built entirely around the student's goals, not sales targets.
