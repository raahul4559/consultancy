    Do you guarantee admission?
    No, we do not guarantee admission to any institution. Our role is to provide comprehensive guidance, help you understand the admission process, and assist with documentation. Admission decisions are made solely by the institutions based on their criteria and available seats.

    Shree Vinayak Consultancy is an independent educational counseling platform. We are not officially affiliated with any college or university unless explicitly stated. Our goal is to provide unbiased guidance to help students make informed decisions.
    Do you provide info on different admission categories?
    Yes, we provide detailed information on various admission categories including management quota, government quota, NRI quota, and institutional quotas. Our counselors can help you understand the eligibility requirements and process for each category.
